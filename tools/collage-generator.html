Portfolio Wallpaper Collage Generator

Instructions

  1. Upload 4-20 photos using the file picker above
  2. Adjust the layout style and settings to your preference
  3. Click "Generate Collage" to create your wallpaper
  4. Click "Download Wallpaper" to save the image
  5. Place the downloaded file in assets/wallpapers/ as default.jpg
  6. Refresh your portfolio to see your new background!

Tip: For best results, use high-quality photos with similar color tones. The overlay helps ensure desktop icons remain visible.
